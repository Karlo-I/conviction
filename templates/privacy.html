Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

Conviction is built on privacy-by-design principles. We collect only the minimum data necessary for the platform to function:

  • Username: Chosen by you at registration (may or may not be your real name)
  • Password hash: Never stored as plain text
  • Quiz responses: To personalize your lens recommendation
  • Contributions and votes: The content you submit to the platform
  • Token transactions: To track your conviction spending

What we do NOT collect

We deliberately do NOT collect:

  • Email addresses: We never ask for or store your email
  • Personal identifiers: No phone number, no real name requirement
  • Tracking data: No analytics, no third-party cookies
  • Location data: Beyond the country codes you voluntarily submit

Account recovery

Important: Because we do not collect email addresses, account recovery is not possible. If you lose your password, you cannot recover your account. Please choose a username and password you will remember.

Data retention

Personal data: If you request account deletion, all personal data (username, password hash, quiz responses, token balance) is deleted within 30 days.

Contributions: Your contributions and votes are retained in anonymized form (username removed) to preserve the integrity of the platform's evidence base.

Infrastructure

Conviction is hosted on Render, which operates under GDPR-compliant data processing terms. No third-party email services are used.

Bot prevention

We use hCaptcha at registration — a privacy-respecting alternative to Google reCAPTCHA that does not send user behavior data to third parties.
